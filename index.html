Italian Pasta

A rich and creamy Italian pasta with tomato sauce and herbs.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Cheese

Preparation:

Boil pasta, sauté tomatoes and herbs, mix together and serve hot with cheese topping.

Veggie Salad

A healthy and fresh vegetable salad perfect for summer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Olive oil & lemon juice

Preparation:

Chop vegetables, drizzle with dressing, and mix well. Serve chilled.

Indian Curry

Spicy and flavorful Indian curry made with traditional spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Mixed spices
  • Paneer or chicken

Preparation:

Sauté onions, add tomatoes and spices, cook the main ingredient, and simmer.
